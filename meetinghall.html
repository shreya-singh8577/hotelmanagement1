Importance of Meeting and Conference Rooms

Business and corporate travelers account for a significant portion of the revenue generated by the travel and hospitality industries. People travel for work all the time — be it to complete an outstation project, attend an international conference or to meet associates and close a deal in another city. With rapid growth in the Indian economy, facilitated by industrial development, many high ranking managers and executives now choose to stay at five star hotels and resorts when traveling to another city for work. Companies also organize conferences, annual meetings and corporate events at some of the most reputed hotels and resorts, to impress clients and investors and thereby promote their brand.

The best hotels and resorts have taken steps to accommodate the needs of businesses and corporate travelers. They provide such guests with important amenities such as:

· A well-equipped business center
· State of the art meeting rooms
· Spacious conference halls for larger events
· Banquet halls for official parties

Well-equipped conference and meeting rooms are an indispensible part of any premium hotel or resort. This is because corporate events and conferences are now extremely common. Events hosted by major companies and corporate bodies are quite prestigious and present an excellent source of revenue for a hotel or resort. Being the venue for a major conference or event helps boost the reputation of a hotel; particularly if the event is attended by dignitaries or well-known personalities in a given field.

Characteristics of the Best Conference Venues

The best meeting rooms and conference venues, offered by the top hotels in Delhi, provide a number of cutting edge facilities to guests. The features of the best conference or meeting rooms are as follows:

· Spacious: The venue must be big enough to comfortably accommodate a large number of guests and attendees, as many corporate events are attended by a huge number of people.

· Location: The hotel must be advantageously located in or near the heart of the city and the conference venue must be well connected to the airport, railway stations, etc.

· Ambience: The venue must offer a chic and professional ambience, with impeccable service and elegant décor, in order to impress the visitors and attendees.

· Facilities: A good conference venue must offer state of the art amenities such as adequate lighting, ventilation, 100% power backup, space for exhibits, lounge areas and outdoor space.

· Technology: The conference venue or meeting room must meet the technical business needs of most industries. It should provide audio-visual equipment, Wi-Fi, computers, etc.

· Service: Excellent, friendly service and catering facilities are also important factors that the best conference venues must take care of.

If you are planning a corporate event or a conference in the national capital, then you should look for the top hotels in Delhi that offer meeting and conference spaces having the above-mentioned features and amenities. You should only book a meeting room or conference venue at a world class luxury hotel with exceptional facilities. By doing so, you will be able to impress your clients and investors while providing the best possible experience to all the guests, visitors and attendees.
